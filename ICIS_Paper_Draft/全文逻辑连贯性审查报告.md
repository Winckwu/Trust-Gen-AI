## 2. Literature Review 逻辑问题（最严重）

### 2.1 整体结构问题
五个小节呈现为**并列关系**，但实际应该是**递进关系**：

```
当前结构（并列）：
2.1 Trust in Automation ——→ 独立介绍
2.2 Trust-Use Paradox ——→ 独立介绍
2.3 Cognitive Offloading ——→ 独立介绍
2.4 Sycophancy Problem ——→ 独立介绍
2.5 Appropriate Reliance ——→ 独立介绍

应有结构（递进）：
2.1 Trust基础 ——因为→ 2.2 出现了悖论 ——其中→ 2.3 技能焦虑是一个维度
                                           ——另外→ 2.4 谄媚是另一个维度
                        ——如何解决→ 2.5 Appropriate Reliance提供了方向
```

### 2.2 具体过渡问题

**2.1→2.2 缺失**：
- 当前：2.1结束于"trust beliefs vs trust behaviors"
- 2.2开头："Emerging evidence suggests..."
- 问题：没有解释为什么从一般性trust讨论转向AI特定paradox

**修复**：
> "The distinction between trust beliefs and behaviors becomes particularly salient in the context of generative AI, where emerging evidence reveals a systematic disconnect between the two."

**2.2→2.3 缺失**：
- 当前：2.2结束于"experienced professional GenAI users"
- 2.3开头："A growing body of research raises concerns..."
- 问题：认知负荷议题从何而来？为什么要讨论？

**修复**：
> "One mechanism that may contribute to this paradox—and that carries its own set of concerns—is cognitive offloading. Users may continue engaging with systems they distrust precisely because these systems have become cognitively indispensable."

**2.3→2.4 缺失**：
- 当前：2.3结束于"represents a gap in current research"
- 2.4开头："A distinct challenge in human-GenAI interaction involves..."
- 问题：谄媚议题与技能退化有什么关系？

**修复**：
> "While skill degradation concerns relate to what users lose through AI reliance, a second tension concerns what users cannot gain: genuine critical feedback. The sycophancy problem represents this distinct but related challenge."

**2.4→2.5 缺失**：
- 当前：2.4结束于"has not been systematically examined"
- 2.5开头："The concept of appropriate reliance (AR)..."
- 问题：为什么突然讨论AR？它如何整合前面的讨论？

**修复**：
> "Given these multiple tensions—the trust-use disconnect, skill degradation fears, and sycophancy frustrations—how might users navigate toward effective human-AI collaboration? The concept of appropriate reliance offers a potential integrative framework."

## 4. Findings 逻辑问题（最严重）

### 4.1 核心问题：4.1与4.2-4.5完全脱节

**当前结构**：
```
4.1 Trust Trajectories: 5种模式
    Rising (38.8%), Declining (16.3%), Steady (14.3%),
    Bell Curve (10.2%), Task-Divergent (20.4%)
    ↓
    [断裂]
    ↓
4.2-4.4 Three Paradoxes
    Trust-Use Decoupling, Skill Anxiety-Dependence, Sycophancy-Skepticism
```

**问题分析**：
1. 38.8% Rising用户去哪了？他们不体现任何paradox吗？
2. 5种trajectory如何"推导出"3个paradox？没有任何解释
3. 4.1看起来像独立的发现，而非4.2-4.4的铺垫

**修复方案**：重写4.1最后一段，建立逻辑桥梁

> "While these trajectory patterns provide a descriptive overview, they mask more complex underlying dynamics. Crucially, users with *different* trajectories often navigated *similar* tensions—and users with *similar* trajectories employed *different* strategies. For instance, both users whose trust rose (Pattern 1) and those whose trust declined (Pattern 2) frequently described wrestling with the same fundamental tensions between reliance and skepticism.
>
> This observation led us to look beyond trajectory categories toward the paradoxical dynamics that characterized sophisticated GenAI engagement across trajectory types. Three interconnected paradoxes emerged from this analysis, cutting across the trajectory patterns described above."

### 4.2 段落间过渡问题

**4.2.1→4.2.2 缺失**：
- 从"High Use Despite Low Trust"到"Trust Ceiling"没有过渡
- 读者不知道为什么突然讨论Trust Ceiling

**修复**：
> "If low trust does not prevent high use, it raises a follow-up question: is there a point at which trust *would* reduce engagement? Our analysis revealed a phenomenon we term the Trust Ceiling—a self-imposed limit suggesting that for many users, no amount of positive experience will generate complete trust."

**4.2→4.3 缺失**：
- 从Trust-Use Decoupling直接跳到Skill Anxiety
- 没有解释这是第二个paradox，也没有说明与第一个的关系

**修复**：
> "The Trust-Use Paradox describes users who continue engaging despite declining trust. But *why* do they continue? One answer lies in a second paradox: users often recognize that their AI reliance may be harming their skills—yet this awareness does not reduce their dependence."

**4.3→4.4 缺失**：同样问题

**修复**：
> "While Paradox 2 concerns what users fear losing (skills), Paradox 3 concerns what they cannot obtain: genuine critical feedback. This tension arises from AI systems' tendency toward sycophancy."

## 5. Discussion 逻辑问题

### 5.1 理论贡献并列问题

**当前结构**：
```
5.1.1 Trust-Use关系重构
5.1.2 Trust Ceiling概念
5.1.3 Skill Anxiety-Dependence Loop
5.1.4 Sycophancy as Trust Signal
5.1.5 Informed Distrust整合
```

**问题**：5个贡献呈并列关系，看不出递进逻辑

**修复方案**：每个小节开头添加过渡

5.1.1→5.1.2：
> "Having established that trust and use can decouple, we now examine a specific mechanism through which users manage this tension: the Trust Ceiling."

5.1.2→5.1.3：
> "The Trust Ceiling explains how users bound their trust. But even with bounded trust, users must still navigate the consequences of their continued engagement—particularly regarding skill preservation."

5.1.3→5.1.4：
> "Beyond skill concerns, users face another tension in their quest for appropriate AI engagement: the challenge of obtaining genuine feedback from systems inclined toward agreement."

5.1.4→5.1.5：
> "These three dynamics—trust-use decoupling, skill anxiety loops, and sycophancy frustration—might seem to describe dysfunction. Yet our analysis suggests they cohere into an adaptive stance we term Informed Distrust."

### 5.2 理论贡献→实践启示过渡缺失

**当前**：5.1.5直接结束，5.2开头是"Our findings suggest several design directions"

**修复**：
> "These theoretical insights carry direct implications for practice. If the paradoxes we identified represent adaptive rather than dysfunctional patterns, then interventions should support rather than suppress them."

### 5.3 实践启示→局限性过渡缺失

**当前**：5.2结束于organizational policy，5.3开头是"Several limitations bound our conclusions"

**修复**：
> "While these implications follow logically from our findings, their generalizability depends on the scope conditions of our study. We now turn to these limitations."

## 总结

论文的核心问题是**"堆砌"而非"建构"**：
- 各部分像独立的砖块，摆放在一起
- 缺乏解释"为什么要讨论这个"、"这与前面有什么关系"
- 读者需要自己推断逻辑关系

学术写作需要**显性化逻辑链条**：
- 每一节开头说明"上一节讲了X，这引出Y问题，本节讨论Y"
- 每一节结尾预告下一节或设置悬念
- 让读者无需思考就能跟随论证流程
